Przemysław Sanecki

Multidisciplinary artist & synthesist.
Also coder, cloud and blockchain architect.
Originally from Poland, he lives and works in Paris and London

Selected Works

Impossible Bodies (2016)
Recurent network, data mining, generative model based on web porn literature, WebGL, browser based installation
Queen of the Dream (2016)
DeepDream network, found footage video, running time 8'08'', HD video installation
Pure Capital (2016)
FX data mining, realtime audiosynthesis & data visualisation AV performance
The Storm that Shelters the Carnival (2016)
FX data mining, realtime audiosynthesis & data visualisation software installation
The Source of the Work of Art (2014)
Generative model of Martin Heidegger texts (MLP), real time CG, 2 channels software installation
Selected works from 2008-2014
