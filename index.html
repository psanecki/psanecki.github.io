Przemyslaw Sanecki
born 1977, PL
lives & works in London, UK

Education

He holds Doctor of Arts degree from University of Arts in Poznan (PL).

Exhibitions

He has exhibited worldwide, including ADAF (Athens, GR), MADAC (Madrid, ES) Pixel (Bergen, NO), Amro (Linz, AT), Slingshot (Athens, US), CSW Zamek Ujazdowski (Warsaw, PL), Galeria Stereo (Warsaw, PL).

List of Works

2014-2016

AI and algorithms based installations and perform.

Impossible Bodies
The work presents short erotic poems written by AI. It tries to break a spell of pseudo-intelligent techno fetish by turning spectators' eyes on an inherent constituent of human spirit spurring from our bodies.
Queen of the Dream
Cognitive technologies are a steam engine of the present, not blotters of LSD. As for that, Queen of the Dream restores a counter-cultural potential of hallucinations
Pure Capital
This performance brings viewers closer to volatile and unpredictable financial phenomenon and offers phenomenological insights into a nature of the economic abstraction.
The Storm that Shelters the Carnival
A software installation which is entirely driven by a real time market data.
The Source of the Work of Art
Will the art emerge as an intrinsic part of machine's subjectivity? The axis of the work is a machinistic interpretation of Martin Heidegger's "Der Ursprung des Kunstwerkes".
