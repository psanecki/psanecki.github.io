PRZEMYSLAW SANECKI

WORKS BIO CONTACT PRESS

Born 1977 in Trzebnica, PL
Lives & works in Edinburgh, UK

Education

  • Doctor of Arts, University of Arts, Poznan, PL (2014)
  • Master of Arts, Academy of Fine Arts,Wroclaw, PL (2005)

Professional

He debuted in 1999 with a performance in Entropia Gallery, Wroclaw, PL.

Since then he is active in many fields of fine arts. His works range from intence body art performance through experimental electronics and live coding practise to novel use of artificial intelligence as medium of art.

His works have been presented both individually and on many ocassions of group shows in art institutions and festivals in Poland, Europe and USA, including Slingshot Festival, Galeria Stereo, Galeria Entropia, Zacheta National Gallery of Art, CSW Ujazdowski Castel, Galeria Start, Sainsbury Centre for Visual Arts in Norwich, and Kunst-Werke in Berlin.
