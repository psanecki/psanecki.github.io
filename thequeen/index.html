The Queen of the Dream maps reality of British politics into a world of dream and illusion and tries to debate a mutual relationship between old political regimes and emerging technocracies.

Recently, deep learning has gained a media attention, mainly due to its spectacular successes in the field of pattern recognition. It has also entered into pop culture thanks to Google's Deep Dream*. Images obtained from trained artificial neural networks send us back to the colorful sixties, when counterculture movements were vital.

However, cognitive technologies are more like a steam engine of the present than blotters of LSD. Psychedelic and ludicrous character of these pictures obscures the fact that the same technologies are essential for ruling classes to maintain their power.
