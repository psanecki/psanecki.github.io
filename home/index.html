PRZEMYSLAW SANECKI

WORKS DATA CONTACT

Data

Born 1977 in Trzebnica, PL
Lives in Edinburgh, UK

Education

  • Doctor of Arts, University of Arts, Poznan, PL (2014)
  • Master of Arts, Academy of Fine Arts,Wroclaw, PL (2005)

Professional

He debuted in 1999 with a performance in Entropia Gallery, Wroclaw, PL. Since then he is active in discipline of fine arts, mainly in fields of performance art, experimental music and computation based art.

He presented his works solo and in many group shows in Poland, Europe and USA, including Slingshot Festival, Zacheta National Gallery of Art, CSW Ujazdowski Castel, Galeria Stereo, Galeria Entropia, Sainsbury Centre for Visual Arts in Norwich, and Kunst-Werke in Berlin.
